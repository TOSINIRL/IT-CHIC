Stuttgart, Germany

Mercedes Benz: 8 Floors of Engineering

I have always wanted to see what the history of the Mercedes Benz looked like. My fascination of old cars and what’s under the hood came from being a tomboy snooping in my dad’s mechanic shop.

"Walking into this building, I felt like a chameleon in a bag of skittles!!!"

I did not know where to start from sheer excitement. 8 floors of automobiles, history, detail and pure black girl joy. The car lover in me was simply elated. I probably took 500 photos. If you make it out to Germany and love cars, this is a must see.

After this visit, for the 2 hour train ride back to Frankfurt I was grinning ear to ear with a happy heart.

There were simply too many photos and pictures say more than I could ever attempt so enjoy the slideshow.
