## üé® IMAGE STYLE GUIDELINES

### Color Palette Consistency
When selecting images, prioritize:
- **Blues**: Ocean, sky, Mediterranean tones (matches primary color)
- **Warm Accents**: Sunset oranges, coral tones (matches accent color)
- **Neutral Bases**: Whites, creams, soft grays
- **Avoid**: Harsh reds, neon colors, overly saturated images

### Lighting
- **Golden Hour**: Warm, soft lighting preferred
- **Natural Light**: Avoid harsh shadows or artificial lighting
- **Consistency**: Similar lighting quality across all images

### Composition
- **Rule of Thirds**: Well-composed, professional photography
- **Negative Space**: Room for text overlays
- **Focus**: Clear subject, not cluttered
